Accessibility Statement

WhenFree is committed to providing an accessible experience for all users, including people with disabilities. This statement outlines our commitment to accessibility and the measures we have taken to ensure our website complies with international accessibility standards.

Accessibility Compliance

Standard:

WhenFree aims to comply with the Israeli Standard IS 5568, which is equivalent to WCAG 2.0 Level AA.

Statement Date:

This statement was last updated on June 2, 2026.

Accessibility Features

WhenFree includes native accessibility features:

  • Keyboard navigation support — navigate the entire app using Tab, Enter, and arrow keys
  • Skip link — press Tab at the top of the page to jump to main content
  • Screen reader support — ARIA labels, landmarks, and semantic HTML for assistive technologies
  • Focus indicators — clear focus rings on all interactive elements
  • Text contrast — minimum 4.5:1 contrast ratio for WCAG AA compliance
  • Responsive design — fully accessible on desktop, tablet, and mobile devices
  • Reduced motion support — respects prefers-reduced-motion media query
  • Multilingual support — available in English, French, and Hebrew with RTL support
  • Accessibility toolbar — floating widget with toggles for text size, high contrast, reduced motion, and more

Accessibility Toolbar

Click the ♿ button in the bottom-right corner to open the accessibility toolbar. This widget provides the following features:

  • Text Size: Choose between Normal, Large, and Extra Large text sizes
  • High Contrast: Increase color contrast and brightness for better visibility
  • Reduce Motion: Disable animations and transitions for users sensitive to motion
  • Focus Highlight: Display bold outlines on focused elements for easier keyboard navigation
  • ADHD Friendly: Reduce visual distractions for users with attention-related needs

Your accessibility preferences are saved and will persist across sessions.

Known Accessibility Issues

We are aware of the following limitations and are working to address them:

  • Calendar grid interactions are primarily mouse-based; keyboard navigation may require improvement
  • Some third-party integrations (Firebase) may have their own accessibility limitations

Browser and Assistive Technology Support

WhenFree is tested and optimized for:

  • Screen readers: NVDA, JAWS, VoiceOver
  • Browsers: Chrome, Firefox, Safari, Edge (latest versions)
  • Devices: Desktop, tablet, mobile (iOS and Android)
