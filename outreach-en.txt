Meet Scheduler: Find the Time. Finally.

WhenFree is an intuitive online meeting scheduler designed to simplify the often-complex process of finding the best time for group events. It eliminates the back-and-forth communication by allowing participants to quickly and easily mark their availability on a shared grid, making it an ideal solution for coordinating schedules efficiently.

This SaaS is perfect for anyone organising group activities, meetings, or social gatherings – from professional teams and community organisers to friends and families – who need to coordinate schedules without the hassle of account creation for participants.

Key Features





Effortless Event Creation: Name your event, select specific dates, and define a time range with ease.



Simple Availability Marking: Participants can quickly drag across a grid to indicate their free times, requiring no account registration.



Intelligent Time Ranking: The application automatically ranks potential meeting slots by how many participants are available, highlighting the optimal times.



Instant Sharing & Access: Share event links via WhatsApp, email, or direct copy; participants can join instantly without needing an account.



Comprehensive Accessibility: Includes features like text size adjustment, high contrast, reduced motion, and an ADHD-friendly mode to reduce distractions.



Organizer Notifications: Organizers can opt to receive email notifications whenever someone responds to their event.
