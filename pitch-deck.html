The Solution

Share a link. Mark availability. Done.

✓
No accounts. No confirmations. No friction. Anyone can join just by opening a link — no app, no signup, no waiting.
✓
Real-time sync — everyone's availability appears live. Powered by Firebase Firestore. As participants mark their times, everyone sees it update instantly.
✓
The app ranks the best times automatically. No manual scanning. WhenFree surfaces the top slots by how many people are free.
"No accounts, no 47-message threads. Just a link. Just answers."
The Product

How WhenFree works

Step 1
📅
Create an event
Name it, pick your dates and time range. Takes 30 seconds.
Step 2
🔗
Share the link
Send via WhatsApp, email, or copy the link. Participants join instantly — no account needed.
Step 3
🔥
Everyone marks their free times
Click or drag on the availability grid. Updates appear live for all participants.
Step 4
⭐
WhenFree finds the best time
Best slots ranked by availability count. Add to Google Calendar or download .ics.

Works on mobile  ·  No app download  ·  No login
